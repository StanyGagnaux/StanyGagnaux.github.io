Recommendation

Our team finished first place in the CFA Institute Research Challenge hosted by CFA Society Switzerland with a maximum grade of 6.0/6.0. We initiated coverage on Sika AG with a BUY recommendation and a CHF 177 twelve-month price target representing a 12% upside from CHF 158 at the time of writing. The thesis was that the market was mispricing Sika as a simple chemical supplier while it is, in substance, a compounder with an unusually deep competitive moat.

Thesis

Three catalysts anchor the call:

  1. A proven M&A playbook: Sika turns fragmented market opportunities into margin expansion; the MBCC integration alone is tracking CHF 200-220m in synergies by 2026.
  2. Exceptional resilience to macro shocks: our stress tests across raw material PPIs, FX, and supply chain pressure showed even 15-year historical extremes moving the stock less than 3%.
  3. Early positioning in data centre infrastructure: a ~CHF 193bn market expected to roughly double by 2029. Sika has already supplied over 1,000 data centres globally.
Approach

We weighted the valuation 80% DCF and 20% relative. The DCF ran on OFCF through 2029 with a 2% terminal growth rate and a WACC of 6.85%, built bottom-up with a regionally weighted risk-free rate and a peer-group beta of 1.13. We also validated it through APV and Equity methods. Bear/base/bull scenarios produced a weighted price range of CHF 156-195, with the base case at CHF 177.

ESG integration: ESG was integrated directly into the discount rate rather than the cash flows: 20 bps added to the market risk premium and 25 bps to the credit spread to reflect Sika's structurally high Scope 3 exposure. Operational ESG progress (renewable electricity at 70.4%, Scope 1+2 down 19.7% vs. 2022) was treated as support for the margin expansion case.
