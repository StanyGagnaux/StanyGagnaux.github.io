Approach

I use the portfolio to stress-test specific investment frameworks through live case studies:

  • Quantitative & systematic approaches Implementing data-driven models to refine entry points and risk parameters.
  • Event-driven & news-based analysis Navigating complex corporate catalysts, such as the strategic merger dynamics and litigation shifts between Fubo and Disney.
  • Contrarian valuation investing Analysing deep-value opportunities in high-scrutiny names like PayPal, specifically focused on identifying the thin line between a valuation trap and a recovery play.
  • Thematic thesis investing Executing high-conviction allocations based on long-term macro shifts, currently centred on the AI semiconductor ecosystem as a primary growth driver.
