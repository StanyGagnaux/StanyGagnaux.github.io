Sustainable lending practices in banks

Evidence from ESG-screened loan portfolios.

Type

Domain

Status

Overview

Examining how banks integrate ESG screening into their lending decisions, and what observable effects this has on portfolio composition, pricing, and credit quality. The work focuses on loan portfolios where ESG filters are explicitly disclosed.
