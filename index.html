About

Background

I am a Swiss national, raised within the French school system in Mozambique. At 17, I made the decision to relocate to France independently to pursue my studies, a transition that demanded immediate cultural adaptability and provided me with a defining sense of self-reliance.

I spent the beginning of my career mastering the art of the client relationship. Whether I was navigating diverse team dynamics as an animator in Mallorca or managing sales advisory roles, I was learning a fundamental truth: finance is a human business.

This realisation led me to my first role in Wealth Management. In this environment, I mastered the essentials of institutional trust: how to ask the right questions, how to present complex solutions with absolute clarity, and how to protect the legacy of the families and entrepreneurs I served.

During my time in Wealth Management, my curiosity shifted from the relationship itself to the quantitative reality that drives it. I became fascinated by the mechanics of how capital is actually deployed, valued, and exited.

I realised that to truly serve a client or a firm, one must bridge the gap between the human objective and the analytical rigour of the markets.

Today, I operate at the overlap of practice, valuation, quantitative analysis, and strategic capital deployment.
